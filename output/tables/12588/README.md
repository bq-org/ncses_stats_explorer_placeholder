# Federal funding for R&D and R&D plant for National defense and Civilian functions: FYs 1955&#8211;2025

**Modal ID:** 12588

**Table Number:** 25

**Type:** TS (Time Series)

**Units:** (Millions of dollars and percent distribution)

## Notes

The National defense function includes Department of Defense military activities and Department of Energy atomic energy defense programs; some years also include defense-related R&D in the Department of Homeland Security and the Department of Justice. Civilian functions include all other federally funded R&D activities.

## Sources

Agencies' submissions to OMB per MAX Schedule C, agencies' budget justification documents, supplemental data obtained from agencies' budget offices, and Executive Office of the President, OMB, Budget of the United States Government, Fiscal Year 2025.
