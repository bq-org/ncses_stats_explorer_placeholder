# Registered USPTO trademarks, by region, country, or economy: 1998–2022

**Modal ID:** 10582

**Table Number:** SINV-35

**Type:** TS (Time Series)

**Units:** (Number)

## Notes

Trademarks are allocated according to holder information. Trademarks are fractionally allocated among regions, countries, or economies based on the proportion of residences of all named holders. China includes Hong Kong. The United States includes Puerto Rico and all other U.S. territories.
