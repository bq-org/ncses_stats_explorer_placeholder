# Companies sampled, by sampling stratum: 2018

**Modal ID:** 2774

**Table Number:** A-4

**Type:** RH (Row Hierarchical)

**Units:** (Number of companies)

## Notes

Companies were said to be known to conduct R&D (known positive R&D) if they reported positive R&D in any of the previous 5 survey years. Companies were said to have known zero R&D if they reported zero R&D in at least 1 of the previous 3 survey years and no positive R&D in any of the previous 5 survey years. Companies were said to be unknown if no R&D information was available.

## Sources

National Center for Science and Engineering Statistics and U.S. Census Bureau, Business Research and Development Survey, 2018.
